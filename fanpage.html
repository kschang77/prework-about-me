Still don't recognize them?

You proobably will after listening to one of their signature songs here...

The band started in 1975 in Australia, but nobody wanted to take a chance on them. Not even opening for Rod Stewart was able to help them crack the American music scene... Eventually their persistence paid off. They cracked the Australia Top Ten in 1980, and their music was finally heard by Clive Davis of Arista Records. And the rest is history. Forty years later, they are still touring.
